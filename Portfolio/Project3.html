Mock Pentesting Project

Within one of my college class our final project was doing a mock pentest on a site and give a proper review on the site.

We created a full topology of the network without knowledge of what devices were there.

We conducted a fake interview with our professor on the company security guidelines as well.

We created a renewed network topology and recomendations on what needed to be done to create a better secure network.

I learned a ton from this final project as we would conduct another actual pen test with the department of commerce in Castle Rock to see if they followed proper guidelines.
